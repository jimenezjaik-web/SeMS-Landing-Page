SeMS Mentor!

Thanks for completing the IATA SeMS Evaluation Test. Congratulations, you are a SeMS Mentor!

You are very well advanced in the subject and seem to have established SeMS fundamentals. To keep up with latest developments we recommend you stay abreast of the most recent publications and news on SeMS. This can also help you and your organization to support development of SeMS in the industry as a whole and mentor those in need of advice and guidance.

In order to maintain your high level of maturity or even bolster your SeMS, you should:

  • Ensure your processes remain consistent and procedures updated
  • Ensure you use your resources effectively to address issues highlighted by outcomes of risk assessment
  • Further promote SeMS with security services providers your organization subcontracts or would like to outsource to in the future
  • Include SeMS principles in your change management processes

To further enhance your SeMS, you should have a look at the solutions below, tied to the advanced level of security of your organization.

Security Management
System Manual (SeMS)

Updated annually, it will help you stay informed about the most recent developments and updates in the scope of SeMS. We expect you already have the latest edition. If that’s not the case, this is the right time to get up to date with the latest SeMS developments.

Integrated Risk and Resilience
Management Manual (IRRM)

It will facilitate your potential internal discussions around integrating many different risk assessment approaches (safety, security, occupational health and safety, etc) and emergency response.

For organizations with advanced SeMS knowledge like yours, AVSEC Insight further elevates their operations.
